§ 01 — Why

Why this matters

"Every enterprise application is now building its own AI. Each one is capable within its own walls. Useless the moment your question crosses them."

SAP Joule. Salesforce Einstein. ServiceNow Now Assist. Microsoft 365 Copilot. Every major platform is shipping AI. Each one only knows its own data. They cannot see each other, cannot share context, and cannot answer questions that span more than one system.

But your business runs across all of them. A customer question touches CRM, ERP and ITSM simultaneously. A weekly performance report needs data from five systems at once. Your siloed AIs cannot help you with that — so you end up doing the work manually, assembling the picture yourself.

EnterpriseHub is the intelligence layer that sits above all of them. One AI with live API connections to every system — so you can finally ask questions no single-app AI was ever designed to answer.
